The Temidire Ultramodern Market Project Ibadan, Oyo State.

Welcome to Golden Bricks Realty

GOLDEN BRICKS REALTY AND INVESTMENT LTD (City Prime Estates Ltd) is a company incorporated in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission in the year 2011 as a real estate development firm with various successes in commercial and residential development across the country with the trademark ‘Premier Garden City’.

Our company is in a public private partnership with the Oyo state government to construct an ultramodern business complex and shopping mall titled TEMIDIRE ULTRA MODERN MARKET IBADAN at Old Ife Road, Gbagi, Ibadan in Egbeda Local Government Area of Oyo State, which will accommodate 1000 lockup shops and several other modern facilities on a BUILD OWN AND OPERATE (BOO} SCHEME. We have the sole right to finance, build and sell the market project to the populace.

Ownership

The company is incorporated with an Authorised Share Capital of 1,000,000 (One Million) with share unit of #1:00 each and is a wholly owned Nigerian entity. The company has two major shareholders that have subscribed as contained in the memorandum and article of associate. The shareholders have nominated seven members as Board of Directors to the company, three from the Oyo State Government and four from City Prime Group. The present equity base of #1,000,000.00 (One Million Naira) and has been fully subscribed.

Vision

The vision is to be a wrold class retail real estate service company in Oyo state, providing shelter for about 10,000 businesses of different classes and interests before the of 2020.

Mission

Our mission is to provide modern market development inform of retail real estate services, made easy to clients on a favourable and affordable payment scheme, thereby making them fulfill their dream of having a shop or business place of their own under a serene, safe and secure environment while we remain sustainable.

Objectives

The core objective of the company is to

Develop acres of land for retail real estate at affordable prices for Oyo State clients under a modern market development scheme known as Neighbourhood Markets.

Equally assist our customers to pay for such retail real estate products under a favourable and flexible payment scheme.

Be sustainable and profitable.

About Us

The Temidire Ultramodern Market (public private partnership) Project Ibadan, Oyo State. We are indeed delighted to formally introduce our ultramodern market project to you our esteemed prospects.

Our partnership with the Oyo State Government require us to form an owner project monitoring team (OPMT/project company) titled TEMIDIRE PRIME INVESTMENTS LIMITED, a Special Propose Vehicle for overseeing the board activity of the project. This Project company is jointly owned by the Oyo State Government and City Prime Estate Limited with the Oyo state government having 30% share of the company while our company is having 70% share . Construction work has commenced on the project site in January, 2018 and has reached seventy percent completion.
